Immigration formalities

Advance Passenger Information is required by the Customs and Border Protection Agency of America and by other countries in advance of the aircraft's departure. Its purpose is to ensure aviation safety and security. To enjoy a quick and easy check-in process, we strongly encourage you to provide this information here before your departure.

You can now enter the USA without a visa via the Visa Waiver Program. This requires an online registration via ESTA. Please register at least 72 hours before your flight departs, in order to receive clearance from the authorities in a punctual manner.
